About

Alex Graham

I am an editorial graphic designer specializing in visual identities, dynamic publication templates, and conscious packaging brand systems. Working at the intersection of historical letterpress crafts and contemporary variable web layouts, my process prioritizes highly tactile physical mediums, clean structural alignments, and refined variable typographic spacing metrics.

By developing bespoke typographic hierarchies and strict alignment structures, I make sure brand systems communicate warmth, structure, and absolute semantic precision.
